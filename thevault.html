Waking Dad (Design Lead)

Imagine Breaking Bad except Heisenberg is your father... and he overdosed... and you have to rescue him from a coma in under a minute by concocting a life-saving potion in his basement laboratory.

Operation Nightfall (Design Lead)

My final year project. Operation Nightfall is what happens when an RTS and a dungeon crawler have a baby while chugging helicopter fuel and smoking crystal cigars during pregnancy.

Hot Steppa (Design Lead)

Platformers. Where innocent characters continually jump rightward, narrowly escaping enemy territory... Fuck that—meet Hot Steppa! Inspired by Nitrome's 'Jack Frost', in this humble yet charming pastime you play as a pissed off pyro-golem. How do you beat a level? By burning it to the ground.

Magno (Design Lead)

Once upon a time, a tiny robot, more damaged than he is adorable, wakes up in a scrap factory. Luckily, he's got the power of positive/negative magnetism in his hands and feet, granting him a whole host of skills that might help him escape.

Cat Jam (Design Lead)

To hell with scrolling mechanisms! This juiced-out rhythm hero thingo has its notes GYRATE! If LoFi were an art style, this is it.

Koala Bi’niss (Director)

The koala mafia are in big trouble when one of their bucks eats a dodgy eucalyptus branch. I had help with the sculpting but did everything else alone: I retopologised the koalas, arranged the scene, wrote the script, set the camera movements, and applied the music, SFX and animations. Okay fine the sculpting I had help with.

Aftermath Cutscene (Director)

A cutscene for a hypothetical video game where two soldiers discover their missing ally dead besides their enemy. As well as creating the entire scene on my own, it comes complete with a branching narrative, fetch quest script, character profiles, and world lore.
